Sacred Games






            Sartaj Singh (Saif Ali Khan) is a troubled police officer in Mumbai
            who receives a phone call from gangster Ganesh Gaitonde (Nawazuddin Siddiqui),
            who tells him to save the city within 25 days. The series chronicles the
            events that follow.
            Starring	        Saif Ali Khan, Nawazuddin Siddiqui, Radhikha Apte
            Genres	        Action and Adventure, Crime
            Audio languages	Hindi
